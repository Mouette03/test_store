
Open Notebook

Open Notebook is an open-source, privacy-first knowledge notebook designed for individuals and teams to capture, organize, and explore ideas efficiently. The application features:

- Local and remote AI assistants for intelligent search, summarization, and content generation
- Secure data storage using SurrealDB, with support for encryption via a customizable key
- Flexible deployment: self-hosted web app, persistent storage, and configurable ports
- Optional web GUI for easy access, or headless mode for automation and scripting
- Integration with local AI models or external APIs (OpenAI, etc.)
- Multi-user support, collaborative editing, and granular access control
- Automatic backups and data export

On Runtipi, Open Notebook is optimized for quick installation, seamless updates, and reliable operation. It is ideal for knowledge management, research, journaling, and collaborative projects, all while keeping your data private and under your control.
