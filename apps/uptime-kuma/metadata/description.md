


## 🇬🇧 English

**Uptime Kuma** is a modern, self-hosted monitoring tool, ready for easy deployment with Runtipi. Effortlessly monitor the uptime of your websites and services (HTTP(s), TCP, Ping, DNS, Docker, and more) with a beautiful, fast, and user-friendly interface.

### ✨ Features

- 🌐 Monitor HTTP(s), TCP, Ping, DNS, Docker, Steam Game Servers, and more
- ⚡ Fast, reactive, and modern UI/UX
- 🔔 90+ notification integrations (Telegram, Discord, Slack, Email, etc.)
- 🗣️ Multi-language support
- 📊 Status pages & ping charts
- 🔒 2FA and proxy support
- 🏷️ Custom domains for status pages
- 📜 SSL certificate info

Perfect for anyone who wants to keep track of their infrastructure’s health directly from their Runtipi dashboard!
